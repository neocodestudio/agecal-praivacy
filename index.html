Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Welcome to Age Calculator! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Age Calculator mobile application.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Age Calculator does NOT collect any personal identifiable information (PII). Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information

1.2 Data You Enter

When you use Age Calculator, you may enter:

  • Birth dates: Dates you calculate ages for
  • Names: Optional names you assign to saved birthdays
  • Theme preferences: Your choice of light or dark mode

Important: All this data is stored ONLY on your device. We never send this information to our servers or any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Storage

All information you enter in Age Calculator is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage system (SharedPreferences). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required to save or access your data
  • Your information is sandboxed and protected by Android's security
  • Other apps cannot access your Age Calculator data

2.2 Data Processing

We use the data you enter solely to:

  • Calculate ages and date differences
  • Display next birthday countdowns
  • Determine zodiac signs
  • Store your saved birthdays for quick access
  • Remember your theme preference

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

Age Calculator uses Google AdMob to display advertisements that help keep the app free. AdMob may collect and process certain information including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, ad interactions
  • Approximate Location: Country or region-level location (not precise GPS location)
  • Ad-related Data: Information about ads you view and interact with

AdMob uses this information to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve advertising services

For detailed information about how Google handles your data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

3.2 Ad Personalization

You have control over ad personalization:

  • Visit your device's ad settings to opt out of personalized ads
  • For Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Learn more at Google Ads Settings

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Local Encryption: Your data is stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms, which are encrypted on devices running Android 6.0 and later
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your data on remote servers, eliminating the risk of server breaches
  • App Sandboxing: Android's sandboxing prevents other apps from accessing your Age Calculator data
  • Secure Connections: All network communication (for ads) uses HTTPS encryption

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data persists as long as the app is installed
  • We have no access to or copies of your data
  • We cannot recover deleted data

5.2 How to Delete Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Individual Birthdays: Swipe or tap the delete button on any saved birthday
  • Delete All Data: Clear app data in Android Settings → Apps → Age Calculator → Storage → Clear Data
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Age Calculator is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will promptly delete such information.

7. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there are no international data transfers of your personal information.

However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of their advertising services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full access to it within the app
  • Right to Delete: You can delete any or all data at any time through the app or Android settings
  • Right to Portability: You can manually export your data by viewing it in the app
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what AdMob collects
  • You can opt out of AdMob's data collection through your device settings

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent (for ads) and legitimate interests (app functionality)
  • Data Controller: Age Calculator App is the data controller for locally stored data; Google is the controller for AdMob data
  • Consent: You provide consent for ad personalization when you install and use the app
  • Withdrawal: You can withdraw consent by adjusting ad settings or uninstalling the app

Age Calculator requires an active internet connection to function. This requirement is solely for:

Your birthday data and calculations do not require internet and are processed entirely on your device.
